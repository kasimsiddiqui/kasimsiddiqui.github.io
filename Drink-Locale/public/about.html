ABOUT LOCALE


THE APP

Discover local Seattle Beers while keeping your fat ass on the couch! Pick a style and get a random beer recommended. Any beer recommended is kept in your "Beer History" for easy reference. Want to see a list of all IPA's? Just check out "Browse"! Find one you like, just ask us to recommend a similar beer and we'll do the rest. We're excited to open your eyes to a whole new world of beer in Seattle.
